Welcome!

Hi! My name is Andrea Alfonzo, and I’m currently an uprising junior in high school. I created Engineering Pathways to help students — like you — navigate the journey to becoming an engineer with clarity, confidence, and creativity.

Why I Chose Engineering

My love for how things work started early. I still remember the moment I discovered that pens have tiny coils inside — that discovery sparked what I now call my “inventor phase.” While I later let go of the idea of becoming an inventor, I found something even more exciting: engineering. It gave me a real path to create things that make life easier.

I’ve always loved problem-solving, especially in math. There’s something satisfying about recognizing patterns — like a difference of squares — and applying them to find elegant and creative solutions. When I learned about Python in high school, I started imagining solutions to everyday problems from my childhood. I sketched ideas for a finger-controlled light switch and dreamed of building a smart system that would turn off my lights without leaving bed. Those ideas didn’t just stay ideas — they evolved into a real curiosity about engineering.

Why I Built This Website

During my freshman year, my dad encouraged me to explore future careers. That’s when it all clicked. Engineering wasn’t just a subject — it was the bridge between childhood imagination and real-world innovation.

But as I started researching, I realized how overwhelming the process was. I didn’t know which classes to take, how to prepare for the SAT, or what opportunities existed outside the classroom — especially as someone who didn’t live in a place with tons of accessible programs. I felt like I had so many ideas, but no roadmap to follow.

What helped me move forward was the idea that there’s no risk in trying — advice from my older brother, who was also starting his own engineering journey. I started researching like crazy: I watched interviews, read articles, planned out my courses, and tackled the SAT one problem at a time. At the gym, I shared my study plan with a friend, and she found it so helpful that I realized something: if I could help one person feel more confident, I could help so many more.

What You’ll Find Here

Engineering Pathways is a collection of everything I’ve learned since freshman year. It includes:

  • Class recommendations by grade level
  • SAT & AP prep resources
  • Project, internship, and extracurricular ideas
  • Scholarship and university tools
  • Articles and interviews to inspire and inform

And although this site is focused on engineering, it’s built to help anyone who wants to challenge themselves and grow — even if they aren’t pursuing engineering.

My Mission

My goal is to make engineering feel accessible, exciting, and achievable. I want students everywhere — no matter their background or school — to know that they can do this. That their ideas matter. That it’s okay to start small, explore slowly, and find your way at your own pace.

This is just the beginning. In the future, I hope to expand the site with a tutoring section, a community submission portal for advice and opportunities, and a stronger Instagram presence to feature weekly interviews with students and engineers around the world.

A Final Note

I hope this site helps you find your footing and your spark.

Thank you so much for being here. This site means a lot to me, and I hope it becomes a place that means something to you, too.
