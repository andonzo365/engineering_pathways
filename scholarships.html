Scholarship Guide

Resources and strategies to help you afford an engineering education.

1. Introduction

Paying for college can be challenging, but there are many scholarships out there for future engineers. This guide will show you where to look, how to apply, and how to stand out.

2. Where to Find Scholarships

  • Bold.org – Niche scholarships, often with quick applications
  • Fastweb – Huge database with filters
  • Niche
  • Scholarships.com – Search by major, state, or deadline
  • Also explore engineering organizations like SWE, NSBE, and SHPE

3. Types of Scholarships

  • Merit-based: For academic achievement, leadership, or special talents
  • Need-based: Based on family income and financial need
  • Identity-based: For underrepresented students (e.g., women in STEM, first-gen, BIPOC, LGBTQ+)
  • Essay/Project-based: Creative or themed writing contests
  • STEM/Field-specific: Scholarships dedicated to engineering and tech majors

4. Application Strategy

  • Start early — many scholarships open in junior year
  • Reuse and refine essays — personalize them for each application
  • Track everything (deadlines and amount) with a spreadsheet or Notion board

6. Final Advice

Be persistent. Apply to everything you qualify for — even small awards add up. Keep your documents organized, stay motivated, and don't compare yourself to others. You don’t need to be perfect — just authentic and driven.

If you ever feel stuck, reach out to a teacher, counselor, or mentor. You’ve got this!
